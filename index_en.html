„24 years of Demeter company (1993-2017)“

Experience

Agricultural trade company Demeter Ltd has been founded in 1993. From the beginning, beside fundamental agricultural production, Demeter has practiced cultivation of vegetables and agricultural services.

Vegetables

Over the years, production of vetegables has gained in importance, finally exceeding the surface of 100 ha (about 250 acres). In January 2012, the company gained a status of a preliminarily approved group of vegetable producers.

Repository

In the plan of being ultimately approved, the company implements a broad investment program. In 2013 the repository capacity of the company reached the level of 4500 ton, including 2000 ton in refrigeration conditions.
